It is duly noted that PC/Konasute Versions are a PAID service

Also known as *Kunasute* versions, SDVX has a few versions that are available to be downloaded and played on PC. Currently, SDVX Exceed Gear is the only live version playable, but before release, SDVX III Gravity Wars also had a Konasute version.

For a more detailed guide and walkthrough on how to set up SDVX for at home play on PC, a link to a step-by-step guide is provided in the See Also section. Here's a quick rundown of the steps:

1.) Create a Japanese Konami/e-amusement account
2.) Download the Launcher for the game here
After launching the game you will see you are limited to 3 days of play and also only 5 songs
3.) Provide payment method to Konami account (credit card)
4.) Purchase the monthly subscription (~$12/month) to play the game here
5.) For more songs here - paywall for additional song packs as well

You are able to bind keyboard and other things you may want to use for your knobs. However, it is best to get a controller as this game has quite the unique controls. The most highly reccommended one is the FAUCETWO with Sanwa buttons, 60g BT button springs and 100g FT button springs.
